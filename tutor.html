The Process

From Application to First Session

Getting started with EduCore is straightforward. Most tutors complete their first session within one week of applying.

Submit your qualifications, subjects, experience, and preferred teaching schedule using our detailed registration form.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find your answer here? Reach us at tutors@educore.ng and we'll respond within 24 hours.

Is there a fee to join EduCore as a tutor? +
No — registration is completely free. EduCore earns through a transparent platform service fee per successful session, which is separate from your agreed hourly rate. You always know your take-home before accepting any booking.
How long does the approval process take? +
Most applications are reviewed within 3 business days. Complex applications requiring additional document verification may take up to 5 days. You'll receive email updates at every stage of the process.
Can I teach online and in-person? +
Yes. EduCore supports both delivery modes. You can set your preference to online only, in-person only, or both, and update your preference at any time through your tutor dashboard. Online sessions use EduCore's built-in video classroom.
What documents do I need to apply? +
You'll need at minimum one academic certificate (degree, diploma, or SSCE), a valid government-issued ID (NIN slip, driver's licence, voter's card, or international passport), and a clear profile photo. Additional certificates strengthen your profile.
How and when are tutors paid? +
Tutors are paid every Friday for all sessions completed in the preceding week. Payments are made via direct bank transfer to your registered Nigerian bank account. You can track all earnings in real-time through your dashboard.
Can I set my own hourly rate? +
Yes. You propose your preferred hourly rate during registration. Our team may advise on competitive benchmarks for your subject and level, but the final rate is agreed by you. You can adjust your rate over time as your reviews grow.
What happens if a student cancels a booked session? +
EduCore's cancellation policy protects tutors. Late cancellations (under 12 hours before a session) trigger an automatic partial compensation to you. Full cancellation terms are covered in the Tutor Code of Conduct.
